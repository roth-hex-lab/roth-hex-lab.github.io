Our project delivers immersive, real-time XR teleguidance for emergency and medical scenarios by combining autonomous drone navigation with semantic 3D reconstruction from aerial sensors. This allows remote operators to gain a precise spatial understanding of complex or inaccessible environments, select points of interest (POI), and guide rescue or medical actions efficiently, bridging robotics, perception, and AI for life-critical applications.
